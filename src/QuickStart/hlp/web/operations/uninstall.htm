Uninstallation


To remove SC-QuickStart from your computer, follow the steps listed below:
  • Close SC-QuickStart.exe
  • Open Start in Windows taskbar
  • Select Settings
  • Click Control Panel
  • Double-click the Add/Remove Programs icon (Win 9X/ME/2000/XP) or the Software icon (Win NT)
  • Select SC-Quickstart from the list
  • Click on the Add/Remove button
  • Continue uninstall procedure
  • Once the uninstall procedure has been completed, SC-QuickStart will have been completely removed from your computer.
