Support


Soft-Central welcomes and appreciates any suggestions, comments and bugreports you might like to provide.
Any questions about SC-QuickStart are welcome too.

Please send an e-mail to support@soft-central.net including:

  1. The symptom of the problem, including any error message.

  2. What operating system you are using.

  3. A description of your computer specifications if you know them (eg RAM, CPU speed, Hard drive size - if known).

  4. Any other information that you feel may be useful to us.

 

We aim to get back to you within 24 hours. Complete customer satisfaction is our No. 1 aim and policy.
